## Change Control Process

1. **Raise** — Document change here with description and rationale
2. **Assess** — PM + Technical Lead evaluate impact on scope, schedule, budget, quality
3. **Approve** — Authority per governance matrix
4. **Implement** — Update affected baseline documents
5. **Communicate** — Notify all stakeholders
