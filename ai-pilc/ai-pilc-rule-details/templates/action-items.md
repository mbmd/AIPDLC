## Status Legend

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ☐ Open | Action assigned; not yet started or in progress |
| 🔄 In Progress | Work underway |
| ✅ Complete | Action finished; outcome documented |
| ❌ Cancelled | No longer required — rationale noted |
| ⏸️ On Hold | Blocked or deferred — reason noted |

---

## Priority Legend

| Priority | Definition |
|:--------:|-----------|
| High | Must be completed before next milestone/gate |
| Medium | Should be completed within 2 weeks |
| Low | Complete when convenient; not blocking |

---

## Governance Rules

1. Actions arise from meetings, reviews, risk responses, and gate decisions
2. Every action has a single owner (person or role)
3. Due dates are mandatory for High priority items
4. Reviewed at every weekly project team sync
5. Overdue actions escalated to PM → Sponsor if blocking
