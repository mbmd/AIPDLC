## Status Legend

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ Final | Decided and approved |
| ☐ Pending | Awaiting input or authority |
| 🔄 Under Review | Options being evaluated |
| ⏸️ Deferred | Postponed to later date |
| ❌ Reversed | Overturned — see superseding entry |

---

## Governance Rules

1. All decisions with scope, budget, or timeline impact must be recorded
2. Numbered sequentially (D-001, D-002, ...)
3. Entries never deleted — only status-updated
4. Reviewed at every Steering Committee meeting
