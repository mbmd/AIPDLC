|--------|---------|
| ☐ Open | Issue identified; resolution in progress |
| ⚡ Escalated | Raised to Steering Committee / Sponsor |
| ✅ Resolved | Issue closed; resolution documented |
| ⏸️ Parked | Monitoring; no action currently needed |

---

## Escalation Criteria

Escalate an issue when:
- Resolution requires decision beyond PM authority
- Issue blocks critical path for >48 hours
- Multiple issues compound and threaten a milestone
- Resource conflict cannot be resolved within the team
