## When to Capture Lessons

- At every phase gate (mandatory reflection)
- When an issue is resolved (what did we learn?)
- When a risk materializes (was our response effective?)
- When a decision is made under uncertainty (what would we do differently?)
- At project closure (formal lessons learned review)

---

## Governance Rules

1. Lessons are captured throughout the lifecycle, not just at the end
2. Every lesson must include a forward-looking recommendation
3. Lessons are reviewed at phase gates and shared with the PMO
4. Negative lessons are welcomed — they drive improvement
5. Entries are never deleted or edited after capture (append-only)
