## Rules

- Entries are sequential and never deleted (even if a decision is later reversed — the reversal gets its own entry)
- Every entry must have a rationale (no bare decisions)
- "Decided By" distinguishes between user's own choice vs. accepting the AI's recommendation
- Pending decisions (deferred) are tracked in `ilc-state.md`, not here — they move here once resolved
